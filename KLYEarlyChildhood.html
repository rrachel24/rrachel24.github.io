Diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education

Study Period:

- 2 years 3 months



Course Content:

- Infant and toddler environment creation and classroom management

- Infant Attachment, Nurturing and Positive Coaching

- Infant Learning through Play

- Behavioral counseling for infants and toddlers

- Art education for preschoolers

- Language Education for Preschoolers

- Early Childhood Development and Growth

- Early Childhood Science Education

- Early Childhood Education Assessment

- Early Childhood Safety, Health, and Nutrition

- Special Children

- Early Childhood Education Research

- Early Childhood Education Teaching Strategies

- Teachers' oral skills and communication skills



Course Entry Requirement:

- GCE "O" level Chinese C6 or above

- HSK 6

- KLY College of Education Chinese Language Test

- SPM / O-Levels: At least three subjects with distinction



Tuition Fee:

- SGD5500 per year
